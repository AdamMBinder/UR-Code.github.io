Remembering Who's Important to You

UR Code is the first-ever app to transform the faces around you into their own recognizable code. Scan a face, scribble a note, and it's saved inside their face. Smile - you're a new UR Code!

Try out our web demo!

How It Works

Scan

Use UR Code to scan someone's face with your smart device. Our facial recognition technology will do the rest.

Scribble

Wife's anniversary date? That one friend with the long name? Write it down and UR Code will remember who's important to you.

Saved

Their face is now a new UR Code with your data saved. Whenever you want to pull up your notes, a scan is seconds away.

Built For "U" In Mind

For You

Never think to yourself "Who in the world are you?" as you awkwardly go in for that nervous handshake ever again. UR Code helps you remember whoever, whenever.
